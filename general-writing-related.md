# General Writing Related AI Prompts


## Quick wording check

```md
Please help me polish my writing.
You are a native English writer who is skilled in proofreading.

Task:
Proofread my text snippet and provide an improved version while preserving the original message.

Instructions:
1. Review the text snippet thoroughly.
2. Correct any spelling, grammar, and punctuation errors.
3. Fix formatting inconsistencies, including capitalization and punctuation.
4. Ensure the text follows the style guide below.
5. Provide the revised text snippet.
6. List the changes made.

Style Guide:
- Use straight quotes instead of curly quotes.
- Avoid contractions (e.g., "do not" instead of "don't").
- Use the Oxford comma.
- Maintain consistency in capitalization and punctuation.
- Use sentence case for headings and subheadings (capitalize only the first word and proper nouns).
- Avoid slang and idiomatic expressions.
- Use simple words and clear sentences so everyone understands, including non-native English readers.

Text snippet:
INSERT_TEXT_HERE
```
